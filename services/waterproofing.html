Professional Waterproofing in Pune

Water damage can cause serious structural issues and health hazards in your property. Manoj Colours provides comprehensive waterproofing services in Pune to protect your home or commercial building from water ingress, dampness, and leakage. With 17+ years of experience, we have successfully solved waterproofing problems for thousands of properties across Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad.

Pune's heavy monsoons make waterproofing essential for every property. Our waterproofing solutions are designed to withstand the harshest weather conditions, providing long-lasting protection for your terraces, walls, bathrooms, basements, and water tanks. We use premium waterproofing products from Dr Fixit, Asian Paints, and other leading brands to ensure the best results.

Why Waterproofing is Essential

  • Prevents Structural Damage: Water seepage weakens the building structure over time.
  • Stops Dampness & Mold: Waterproofing prevents mold growth that can cause health issues.
  • Protects Interiors: Prevents paint peeling, plaster damage, and interior deterioration.
  • Increases Property Value: A waterproofed property has higher resale value.
  • Saves Money: Prevents costly repairs caused by water damage.
  • Peace of Mind: Enjoy monsoons without worrying about leaks and dampness.

Our Waterproofing Services

  • Terrace Waterproofing: Complete terrace waterproofing with heat-reflective coatings.
  • External Wall Waterproofing: Protection for exterior walls against rain penetration.

Our Waterproofing Process

  1. Inspection & Diagnosis

    Thorough inspection to identify the source and extent of water ingress.

  2. Surface Preparation

    Cleaning, removing loose material, and preparing the surface for treatment.

  3. Crack Treatment

    Sealing cracks and joints with specialized waterproofing compounds.

  4. Primer Application

    Applying waterproofing primer for better adhesion.

  5. Waterproofing Coating

    Application of multiple coats of waterproofing material.

  6. Quality Testing

    Water ponding test to ensure complete waterproofing.

FAQs About Waterproofing

How long does waterproofing last?

With proper application and quality materials, waterproofing can last 8-10 years. We provide up to 10 years warranty on our waterproofing work.

Can waterproofing be done during monsoon?

Waterproofing requires dry conditions for proper curing. We recommend doing it before or after the monsoon season for best results.

Do you provide warranty?

Yes, we provide up to 10 years warranty on waterproofing services, covering any leakage or dampness issues.
