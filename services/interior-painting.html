Our Interior Painting Process

We follow a systematic 6-step process to ensure flawless results on every interior painting project:

  1. Site Inspection & Measurement

    Our team visits your home to assess the condition of walls, measure the area, and understand your requirements.

  2. Color Consultation

    We help you select the perfect colors and finishes based on your preferences, lighting conditions, and room usage.

  3. Surface Preparation

    We clean the walls, fill cracks and holes, sand rough areas, and apply primer for optimal paint adhesion.

  4. Protection & Masking

    We carefully cover furniture, floors, switches, and fixtures to protect them from paint splatter.

  5. Paint Application

    Our skilled painters apply the paint using professional techniques for a smooth, even finish.

  6. Cleanup & Final Inspection

    We remove all protective coverings, clean the area, and conduct a final inspection with you.

FAQs About Interior Painting

How long does interior painting take?

A standard 2BHK apartment typically takes 4-6 days to complete. The timeline depends on the size of the area, number of rooms, and the condition of walls. We provide a detailed timeline during the quotation phase.

Do I need to move my furniture?

We recommend moving small items and valuables. Our team will help cover and protect larger furniture pieces. We take every precaution to protect your belongings during the painting process.

What type of paint do you recommend for interiors?

We recommend Asian Paints Royale, Dulux Velvet Touch, or Birla Opus premium range for interiors. These paints offer excellent coverage, durability, and washability. Our experts can recommend the best option based on your specific needs and budget.

Is there a smell after painting?

We use low-VOC, odorless paints that have minimal smell. Any slight odor typically dissipates within 24-48 hours with proper ventilation.
