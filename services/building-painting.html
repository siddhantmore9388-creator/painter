Building Painting Contractor in Pune

Manoj Colours is a leading building painting contractor in Pune, specializing in complete exterior and interior painting solutions for residential buildings, apartment complexes, and high-rises. With 17+ years of experience and a proven track record of over 500 successful building painting projects, we have the expertise, equipment, and manpower to handle projects of any scale.

Building painting requires specialized skills, proper scaffolding, safety equipment, and coordination. Our team is trained in high-rise painting techniques and follows strict safety protocols to ensure smooth execution and flawless results. We understand the unique challenges of building painting in Pune's climate and use weather-resistant paints that provide long-lasting protection.

Why Choose Us for Building Painting?

  • Specialized Equipment: We arrange proper scaffolding, gondolas, and safety equipment for high-rise buildings.
  • Experienced Team: Our painters are trained in building painting techniques and safety protocols.
  • Complete Solutions: From exterior facades to common areas, staircases, and parking areas.
  • Weather-Resistant Paints: Premium exterior paints that withstand Pune's monsoons and heat.
  • Society Coordination: We work closely with society management to minimize resident inconvenience.
  • Insurance Coverage: Full insurance for workers and property during the project.

Building Painting Services We Offer

  • Exterior Facade Painting: Complete exterior painting including walls, columns, and architectural features.
  • Common Area Painting: Lobbies, corridors, staircases, and lift areas.
  • Painting & Marking: Parking areas, stilt floors, and basement painting.
  • Boundary Walls: Compound walls, gates, and security cabins.
  • Water Tank Painting: Internal and external painting of overhead and underground tanks.
  • Repair & Maintenance: Crack filling, waterproofing, and surface repairs.

Our Building Painting Process

  1. Site Survey & Assessment

    Comprehensive inspection of the building to assess surface conditions and identify repair needs.

  2. Quotation & Approval

    Detailed quotation with scope of work, timeline, and payment terms for society approval.

  3. Scaffolding Setup

    Installation of safe and sturdy scaffolding or gondola systems for high-rise access.

  4. Surface Preparation

    High-pressure washing, crack repairs, and primer application.

  5. Paint Application

    Professional painting using premium weather-resistant exterior paints.

  6. Final Inspection & Handover

    Quality check with society representatives and warranty documentation.

Building Painting Cost in Pune

Building painting costs depend on the building height, total surface area, paint quality, and scope of work. Here are approximate rates:

  • Small Building (G+4): ₹3-8 Lakhs
  • Mid-Rise (G+7 to G+12): ₹8-20 Lakhs
  • High-Rise (G+15+): ₹20-50+ Lakhs
