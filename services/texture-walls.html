Texture Wall Painting Services in Pune

Add depth, character, and artistic flair to your interiors with Manoj Colours' texture wall painting services in Pune. Texture painting transforms ordinary walls into stunning focal points that reflect your personal style and elevate your home's aesthetics.

With 17+ years of experience in decorative painting, our skilled artisans can create a wide range of textures - from subtle and elegant to bold and dramatic. Whether you want a feature wall in your living room, a textured backdrop for your bedroom, or creative designs throughout your home, we bring your vision to life with precision and artistry.

Why Choose Texture Wall Painting?

  • Unique Aesthetics: Create one-of-a-kind walls that stand out and impress.
  • Hides Imperfections: Textures effectively conceal minor wall defects and unevenness.
  • Adds Depth: Creates visual interest and dimension in your interiors.
  • Durable: Texture paints are more durable and easier to maintain than flat paints.
  • Versatile: Available in countless designs to match any decor style.
  • Value Addition: Enhances the overall value and appeal of your property.

Texture Options We Offer

  • Royal Play: Asian Paints' premium texture range with stunning metallic and special effects.
  • Stencils: Precise patterns and designs for elegant feature walls.
  • Sponge Texture: Soft, cloud-like patterns perfect for bedrooms.
  • Rag Rolling: Classic textured finish with a sophisticated look.
  • Comb Texture: Linear patterns that add modern elegance.
  • Brick & Stone Effects: Realistic textures that mimic natural materials.
  • 3D Wall Panels: Dramatic dimensional effects for statement walls.
  • Metallic Finishes: Luxurious shimmer and shine for premium spaces.

Our Texture Painting Process

  1. Design Consultation

    We discuss your preferences, show samples, and help you choose the perfect texture for your space.

  2. Surface Preparation

    Thorough wall preparation including cleaning, sanding, and primer application.

  3. Base Coat Application

    Application of base coat that forms the foundation for the texture.

  4. Texture Creation

    Our artisans skillfully create the chosen texture using specialized tools and techniques.

  5. Top Coat & Finishing

    Application of protective top coat for durability and the desired sheen level.

  6. Final Inspection

    Quality check and touch-ups to ensure perfection.

FAQs About Texture Painting

How long does texture painting take?

A single feature wall typically takes 2-3 days. Multiple rooms or complex designs may take 5-7 days.

Can texture be applied to any wall?

Yes, but the wall needs to be in good condition. We assess and prepare the surface before application.

How do I maintain textured walls?

Textured walls are easy to maintain. Simply dust regularly and clean with a soft, damp cloth when needed.
