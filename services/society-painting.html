Society Painting Services in Pune

Maintaining the aesthetic appeal and structural integrity of housing societies requires professional painting services that understand the unique challenges of community living. Manoj Colours offers comprehensive society painting services in Pune, catering to housing societies, apartment complexes, and residential communities of all sizes.

With 17+ years of experience and hundreds of successful society painting projects, we have established ourselves as the preferred painting contractor for housing societies across Pune and Pimpri Chinchwad. Our team understands the importance of minimal disruption to residents, proper coordination with society management, and delivering quality results that enhance the value of your property.

Our Society Painting Services

  • Exterior Facade Painting: Complete exterior painting of society buildings including walls, columns, and architectural elements.
  • Common Area Painting: Lobbies, corridors, staircases, lift areas, and clubhouse painting.
  • Parking Area Painting: Stilt parking, basement, and open parking area painting with markings.
  • Compound Walls & Gates: Painting and maintenance of society boundary walls and entrance gates.
  • Water Tank Painting: Internal and external painting of overhead and underground water tanks.
  • Repair & Maintenance: Crack filling, waterproofing, and surface repairs before painting.

Why Societies Choose Us

  • Resident-Friendly Approach: We work in phases to minimize inconvenience to residents.
  • Proper Coordination: Regular communication with society management committee.
  • Quality Materials: Premium weather-resistant paints for long-lasting results.
  • Safety First: Full scaffolding, safety equipment, and insurance coverage.
  • Competitive Pricing: Special rates for society projects with transparent quotations.
  • AMC Available: Annual maintenance contracts for regular upkeep.

Our Society Painting Process

  1. Initial Meeting

    Meeting with society management to understand requirements and scope of work.

  2. Site Survey

    Detailed inspection of all areas to be painted and identification of repair needs.

  3. Quotation & Approval

    Detailed quotation presented to the management committee for approval.

  4. Resident Communication

    Notices to residents about work schedule and any temporary arrangements.

  5. Execution

    Professional painting execution with regular progress updates.

  6. Final Inspection & Handover

    Joint inspection with committee members and warranty documentation.

FAQs About Society Painting

How long does society painting take?

Depending on the society size, it typically takes 1-3 months. We work in phases to ensure minimal disruption to residents.

Do you handle all permissions?

We assist with all necessary documentation and can coordinate with authorities if any permissions are required for scaffolding.

Can residents stay during painting?

Yes, residents can stay. We work on exteriors and common areas, ensuring individual flats remain accessible.
