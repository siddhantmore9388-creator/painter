Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our painting services

How much does home painting cost in Pune?

The cost of home painting in Pune depends on several factors including the size of your home, type of paint chosen, surface condition, and the number of coats required. On average, interior painting costs range from ₹12-25 per sq ft, while exterior painting ranges from ₹15-30 per sq ft. Contact us for a free detailed estimate.

How long does it take to paint a house?

A standard 2BHK apartment typically takes 4-6 days to complete including preparation, painting, and drying time. Larger homes or commercial projects may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the quotation phase and ensure timely completion.

What paint brands do you recommend?

We recommend and use premium brands like Asian Paints, Dulux, and Birla Opus. The best choice depends on your budget, requirements, and the specific surface. Our experts can guide you in selecting the right paint for optimal results and durability.

Do you provide a warranty on your painting work?

Yes, we provide up to 5 years warranty on our painting services. This covers peeling, flaking, and fading under normal conditions. We use only genuine paints and follow proper application procedures to ensure long-lasting results.

Do I need to vacate my home during painting?

No, you don't need to vacate your home. We use low-VOC, odorless paints that are safe for occupants. Our team works in a systematic manner, completing one room at a time so you can continue using other areas of your home.
